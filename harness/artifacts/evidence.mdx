Every accepted artifact has an evidence trail. The trail combines:

- the `artifactRecord` itself
- the `runEvent` stream that produced it
- the `policyDecision` records that gated it
- the `toolExecution` records that built it
- the `auditEvent` envelope tying them together
- the `evidencePacket` emitted at closeout

## Audit honesty

The evidence trail is the gate between code-verifiable claims and human-publishable claims. See the [Release readiness](/harness/release/readiness) page for the matrix of supported and unsupported public claims.
