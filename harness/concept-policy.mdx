**Policy** is the seam that decides what an agent is allowed to do. Jami Harness evaluates
identity, scopes, and consent boundaries before tool side effects occur, and routes anything
that needs a human decision through [approvals](/harness/concept-approvals).

## Default Decisions

The default engine denies malformed requests, missing actor/project/environment context,
missing required scopes, unsupported risks, secret values in policy requests, and threat
signals such as prompt injection, tool metadata poisoning, and secret exfiltration.

## Risks

Supported risks are `read`, `write`, `destructive`, `external`, `secret_adjacent`, and
`unknown`. Elevated risks require a current approval unless a custom policy engine changes
that behavior.

## Replaceable Seam

Pass `createHarness({ policyEngine })` to replace policy evaluation while preserving the
same run, approval, audit, and evidence contracts.
