Approvals are how Harness keeps a human in the loop for high-risk actions without breaking the run contract.

## Approval envelope

Every approval flows through `approvalRequest`, which carries:

- the action ref being approved
- the approver scope
- the policy decision that surfaced the approval
- the audit event ids tying the request to the run trail

## Modes

- **Implicit** — covered by scope and risk labels; no human prompt.
- **Explicit** — a human approval is required before the tool call proceeds.
- **Deferred** — the run pauses at a checkpoint until approval is recorded.

Approval modes are declared per tool and per primitive in capability manifests.
