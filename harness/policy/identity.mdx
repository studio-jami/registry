Identity and governance are owned-core mechanism areas. Harness models actors, tenants, projects, environments, and roles directly; adapters resolve them but do not redefine them.

## Identity model

- **Actor** — the entity initiating an action (user, agent, system).
- **Tenant** — the trust boundary the actor belongs to.
- **Project** — a workstream context owned by a tenant.
- **Environment** — dev/stage/prod-style context for policy scoping.
- **Role** — a named set of scopes assigned to actors.

## Secret resolution

Secrets are never written into tracked output. They are referenced through `secretRef`, which names the secret without committing the value. Generated route bundles and release manifests list secret names and required human actions; they never include connection strings or tokens.
