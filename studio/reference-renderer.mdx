Jami Studio components are available through two rendering layers: a framework-neutral
factory that produces plain element descriptors, and pre-built React/Radix wrappers. This page
will document both export surfaces.

## Planned reference

- `renderPrimitiveSpec(name, props)` and `createJamiPrimitiveComponents(createElement)`.
- The React/Radix `forwardRef` wrappers (`JamiButton`, `JamiPanel`, and others).
- The component vocabulary, prop schemas, token requirements, and ARIA contracts.
