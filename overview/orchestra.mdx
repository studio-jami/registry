**Jami Orchestra** is the planned multi-agent orchestration layer for the Jami Studio family.
Where [Harness](/overview/harness) governs a single agent's run, Orchestra's goal is to
coordinate *many* runs — sequencing work across agents, sharing context and artifacts, and
keeping the whole effort resumable and auditable.

## Intended direction

- Coordinate multiple Harness runs toward a shared goal.
- Reuse the Harness contracts (runs, tasks, tools, policy, artifacts, evidence) rather than
  inventing a parallel model.
- Keep the same governance posture: explicit policy, approvals, and full provenance.

## Status

Orchestra is tracked as a vision stub for launch. It will be promoted to a full
documentation section once it has a real, installable surface to document.
